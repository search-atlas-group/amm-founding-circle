# Voice Profile: Acme Home Services

## Tone words
direct, no-nonsense, friendly, local

## Banned phrases
- "synergy"
- "leverage our solutions"
- "in today's fast-paced world"
- "at the end of the day"
- "circle back"

## Reading level
Grade 6-8 (plain language, homeowners reading on their phone, no jargon)

## Formatting rules
- Use the Oxford comma
- No em-dashes in headlines — use a colon or period instead
- Sentence case for headlines, not Title Case
- Contractions are fine ("we'll", "you're") — this brand talks like a person

## Sounds like us
- "We show up on time, fix it right, and tell you exactly what we did."
- "No surprise fees. If something changes, you hear about it before we start."

## Doesn't sound like us
- "We leverage synergistic solutions to optimize your home's operational efficiency."
- "At the end of the day, our team is committed to circling back on all deliverables."
