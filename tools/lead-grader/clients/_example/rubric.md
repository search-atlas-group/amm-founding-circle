# Example Client Name — Lead Scoring Rubric

This is a **sample rubric** so you can see the shape the wizard produces
(`python3 run.py --client <slug> wizard`). Delete this file and run the
wizard on your own 5-10 labeled example calls instead of hand-editing
this — the wizard writes exactly this shape for you.

## Hot 🔥
The caller is a real prospect, in the service area, with a concrete need
and a timeline of a month or less. e.g. "I noticed a leak yesterday and
want a quote for a full replacement" — a specific problem, ready to act.

## Qualified ✅
A real prospect with genuine interest but a softer timeline or a
question still open (budget, scope, or "just comparing quotes").

## Weak ⚠️
Genuine interest but too little signal to act on yet — vague timeline,
"just curious," or a renter/non-decision-maker asking hypothetically.

## Junk 🗑️
Not a real lead at all: wrong number, a vendor/solicitor pitch, a
recruiting call, or anything with zero buying intent.
