## Decision rule (set before you start)
- Each model scores every criterion PASS or FAIL and gives one line of reasoning.
- A criterion that any model fails is **surfaced**, not averaged away.
- The version to ship is the one that passes all criteria across the majority of models.
- If the models split on a criterion, **you** break the tie — with their disagreement in front of you.
