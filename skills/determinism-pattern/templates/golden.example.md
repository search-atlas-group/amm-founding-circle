# Weekly SEO Report

Client: Acme Plumbing
Week of: 2026-06-23

## Summary

Organic traffic is up 12% week over week, driven mainly by the three service
pages we rewrote last month. Rankings held on every priority keyword and we
picked up two new page-one positions. Nothing is on fire; the plan is working.

## What moved

- "emergency plumber [city]" climbed from position 8 to 4 — the rewrite plus the
  two new local citations did it.
- The booking page's load time dropped from 4.1s to 2.3s after we compressed the
  hero image, which lifted its conversion rate.
- One older blog post lost a position; a competitor refreshed theirs. Low stakes,
  but we're watching it.

## Next steps
